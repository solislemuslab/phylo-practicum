**Learning goal:** How do we go from local signals of introgression (triplets/windows) to claims about historical hybridization between clades?

## Activity 1: HyDe Triplet Mining (20 min)

Let's look in groups at the HyDe output tables. We want to find strong signals of hybridization:
- Which triplets have hybridization index far from 0 or 1?
- Which taxa repeatedly appear as "hybrids"?
- Does the same taxon show signals with different parental pairs?
- Are there contradictions?

If all these signals were true simultaneously, what kind of evolutionary history do we need to explain?
