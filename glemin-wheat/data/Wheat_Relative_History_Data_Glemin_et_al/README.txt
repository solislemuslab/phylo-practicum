GENE LIST
1) MappedOnHordeumChromosomes_OneCopyGenes.txt
List of one-copy genes with their coordinates on the Hordeum genome


ALIGNMENTS
2) IndividualAlignements_OneCopyGenes.zip
Individual alignment for all one-copy genes
3) FullConcatenation_OneCopyGenes.zip
Concatenation of all one-copy genes
4) Concatenation10Mb_OneCopyGenes.zip
Alignements corresponding to the concatenation of genes over 10Mb windows

TREES
5) MLtree_OneCopyGenes.tree
ML tree of the concatenation
6) MLtree_OneCopyGenes_ultrametric.tree
Ultrametric version of the same tree
7) Densitree_OneCopyGenes.nex
Liste of 10Mb ML trees used for the densitree plot

HYDE RESULTS
8) HydeResults_OneCopyGenes.csv
List of Hyde results for all possible triplets of individuals
- Hind: focal individual
- P1ind: parent1 individual
- P2ind: parent2 individual
- Hspecies: species of focal individual
- P1species: species of parent1 individual
- P2species: species of parent2 individual
- HillStat: Hill's statistics
- Pvalue: corresponding p-value (no correction)
- gamma: proportion of parent2
- Nsnp: total number of SNPs used for the given triplet
- Topo1: # of SNPs supporting ((P1,H),P2)
- Topo2: # of SNPs supporting ((P2,H),P1)
- Topo3: # of SNPs supporting ((P1,P2),H)
